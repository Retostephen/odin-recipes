Pizza Soup

Recipe Description

Ingredients

These are the Ingredients you'll need to make this pizza soup recipe:

  • 1 (3 1/2 ounce) package sliced pepperoni
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 green ball pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 teaspoons dried Italian seasoning, crushed
  • 1 cup shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cut pepperoni slices in half
  3. COok pepperoni in a 6-quart soup pot over medium-high heat untill pepperoni starts to crisp, stirring occasionally, about 5 minutes.
  4. Remove pepperoni from pan and drain on paper towels. Set aside.
  5. In the same pot, cook sausage over medium-high heat untill cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add onion and peppers; cook untill crisp-tender, stirring occasionaly, about 5 minutes.
  7. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, beef broth and Italian seasoning to the pot. Bring mixture to boiling over medium high heat. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Top each serving with crispy pepperoni, mozzarella, and Parmesen cheese.
